About Us

Welcome to HausBook! We have developed a web app where you can get localized information within a 30-mile radius that includes live current events, local news, local politics, local small businesses information. The app also acts as a neighborhood social platform where you can join your neighborhood group and communicate with those within your community through messaging/forum. It can also provide information on local weather.
